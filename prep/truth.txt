<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="By_type_of_notation">By type of notation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_numeral_systems&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By type of notation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Numeral systems are classified here as to whether they use <a href="/wiki/Positional_notation" title="Positional notation">positional notation</a> (also known as place-value notation), and further categorized by <a href="/wiki/Radix" title="Radix">radix</a> or base.
</p>

<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Binary_clock.svg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/27/Binary_clock.svg/220px-Binary_clock.svg.png" decoding="async" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/27/Binary_clock.svg/330px-Binary_clock.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/27/Binary_clock.svg/440px-Binary_clock.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="539" data-file-height="395" width="220" height="161"></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Binary_clock.svg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>A <a href="/wiki/Binary_clock" title="Binary clock">binary clock</a> might use <a href="/wiki/Light-emitting_diode" title="Light-emitting diode">LEDs</a> to express binary values. <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Non-positional_notation">Non-positional notation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_numeral_systems&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Non-positional notation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>All known numeral systems developed before the <a href="/wiki/Babylonian_numerals" class="mw-redirect" title="Babylonian numerals">Babylonian numerals</a> are non-positional,<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">[34]</a></sup> as are many developed later, such as the <a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">Roman numerals</a>.  The French Cistercian monks created <a href="/wiki/Cistercian_numerals" title="Cistercian numerals">their own numeral system.</a>
</p>
